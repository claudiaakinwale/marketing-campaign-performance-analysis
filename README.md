# Marketing Campaign Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes marketing campaign performance across multiple digital marketing channels using Power BI. The dashboard helps business stakeholders monitor campaign performance, identify high-performing marketing channels, understand customer segments, and improve marketing return on investment (ROI).

## Business Problem

Marketing teams often invest heavily across multiple advertising channels but struggle to identify which campaigns generate the best business outcomes. Without a centralized dashboard, decision-makers find it difficult to monitor KPIs and optimize marketing spend.

---

## Project Objectives

- Monitor marketing performance
- Compare campaign effectiveness
- Analyze customer segments
- Track ROI and conversion rates
- Support data-driven decision making

## Dashboard Pages

### Executive Dashboard
Provides a high-level overview of:

- Total Impressions
- Total Clicks
- Conversion Rate
- ROI
- Acquisition Cost

### Campaign Performance Dashboard

Provides deeper insights into:

- Customer Segments
- Marketing Channels
- Geographic Performance
- Business Recommendations

---

## Key Insights

- Google Ads generated the highest overall impressions.
- Customer conversion rates remained relatively consistent across customer segments.
- ROI remained stable across campaigns.
- Marketing performance varied by location.
- Acquisition costs differed by advertising channel.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Increase investment in high-performing channels.
- Optimize underperforming campaigns.
- Personalize marketing for different customer segments.
- Monitor campaign performance continuously.
- Use data-driven decision making.
